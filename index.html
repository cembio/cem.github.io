Services

Fixation

We use various fixation protocols and chemicals to various sample types ensuring maximal preservation of ultrastructural details.

Embedding

For embedding of biological specimens (e.g. cell culture, biopsies or tissues) we use various resins adjusted to specific fetaures of samples.

Sectioning

We make semifine sections (1-2 µm) for localization of specific tissue or group of cells of interest (by staining or immunohistochemistry). Ultrafine sections (50 nm) are mounted onto metal grids (Cu, Ni, Pd/Au). All these section procedures are done on ultramicrotome.

Contrasting

Making ultrastructural details “be visible” on electron microscope. This procedure is done with automatic stainer.

Imaging

It is possible to observe ultrastructural details, describe pathological processes in cell and organelles and another analyses. All observed structures and micrographies are taken by digital camera.
